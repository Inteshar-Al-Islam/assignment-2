Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Focuses on foundational learning and social skills through play-based activities. It nurtures creativity and emotional development in a supportive setting.

Elementary School

Develops core academic skills and fosters curiosity in a structured environment. Emphasizes the importance of teamwork and independent learning.

Middle (10-16) Years

Prepares students for higher education with advanced subjects and critical thinking development. Encourages self-awareness and exploration of personal interests.
